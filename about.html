Our Story

Hey there food lovers! We're the Foodie family, your friendly neighborhood food enthusiasts hailing from the picturesque city of Edinburgh, UK. We're just your regular folks with an extraordinary passion for food. But not just any food, mind you! We're all about delivering the freshest, healthiest, and most mouthwatering dishes right to your doorstep.

Foodie is our own restaraunt but we can facilitate your order from other restaraunts too! Just use the search bar to enter your post code and choose to browse one of our loal partnered establishments or one of our foodie chains that specialise in Greek cuisine

Our journey began with a shared love for Greek cuisine. Picture this: warm Mediterranean breezes, sun-kissed olive groves, and a table laden with delectable Greek delights. It was love at first bite, and we couldn't help but bring that love back home to our fellow foodies.
And yes, Gordon Ramsay came to our first restaurant opening!

Our Mission

Our mission at Foodie is simple: to provide you with the best of both worlds. We're dedicated to delivering restaurant-quality, healthy meals directly to your door. But wait, there's more! We're not just about delivering delicious food; we're also here to share the secrets behind our culinary creations.

We're not hoarding our recipes like a family secret. Nope! We're sharing them with you. Check out our recipe section (coming soon) for step-by-step guides on recreating your favorite dishes at home. Get ready to impress your friends and family!
